Connecting Students with Meaningful Volunteer Opportunities

Buliga is a platform designed to bridge the gap between schools and students, making it easier than ever to find and participate in volunteer activities.

How It Works

Schools Post Opportunities

Schools create and publish volunteer opportunities, detailing the tasks, requirements, and schedules.

Students Browse and Apply

Students explore available opportunities, filter by interests, and apply to the ones that match their skills and availability.

Schools and Students Connect

Schools review applications, communicate with students, and coordinate volunteer activities.
